Project 3: Customer Revenue Intelligence (Excel)
Overview
This project analyzes simulated retail sales transactions to evaluate revenue performance by product, membership type, and region. The goal is to generate actionable business insights using Excel.

Key Results
Total Revenue: $436,030
Top Product by Revenue: Jacket ($139,920)
Top Membership by Revenue: Regular ($248,295)
Top Region by Revenue: East ($134,720)
Cross Insight: Regular members drove the highest Jacket revenue ($74,880)
Business Insights
Jackets are the primary revenue driver, indicating strong demand and pricing power.
Regular members contribute the largest share of revenue, suggesting growth is currently volume-driven.
East leads regional performance and may be the strongest market for targeted campaigns.
High-value product demand is broad-based, not limited to premium tiers.
Strategic Recommendation
Strengthen loyalty incentives and upsell pathways to convert high-spending Regular customers into Silver/Gold tiers to improve long-term customer value.
